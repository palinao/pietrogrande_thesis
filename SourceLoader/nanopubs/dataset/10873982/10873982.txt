Genetic screening in a large family with juvenile onset primary open angle glaucoma.

Authors: 
Booth AP, Anwar R, Chen H, Churchill AJ, Jay J, Polansky J, Nguyen T, Markham AF

A number of genetic loci have been implicated in the pathogenesis of primary open angle glaucoma (POAG). The aim of this study was to identify the genetic cause of POAG in a large Scottish family and, if possible, offer genetic screening and advice to family members.
