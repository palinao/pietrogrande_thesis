Dot-and-fleck retinopathy in Alport syndrome caused by a novel mutation in the COL4A5 gene.

Authors: 
Blasi MA, Rinaldi R, Renieri A, Petrucci R, De Bernardo C, Bruttini M, Grammatico P

To describe an unusual form of dot-and-fleck retinopathy in a slower progressive form of X-linked Alport syndrome, caused by a novel missense mutation in the COL4A5 gene.
