A novel de novo mutation in the desmin gene causes desmin myopathy with toxic aggregates.

Authors: 
Sugawara M, Kato K, Komatsu M, Wada C, Kawamura K, Shindo PS, Yoshioka PN, Tanaka K, Watanabe S, Toyoshima I

To determine the cause and pathogenic mechanisms of a 21-year-old patient's cardioskeletal myopathy. The patient's muscle atrophy and weakness began in distal parts of limbs; cardiac and facial muscles were later involved.
