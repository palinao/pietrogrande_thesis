Characterization of a novel missense mutation in the pore of HERG in a patient with long QT syndrome.

Authors: 
Yoshida H, Horie M, Otani H, Takano M, Tsuji K, Kubota T, Fukunami M, Sasayama S

A new strategy to elucidate the molecular mechanisms underlying the long QT syndrome (LQTS) is now available with genetic mutational analyses and characterization of ion channel mutations.
